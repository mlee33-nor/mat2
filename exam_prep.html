Cheat Sheet Print-friendly

Exponential $f(x)=b^x$

  • Domain $(-\infty,\infty)$, Range $(0,\infty)$
  • Asymptote $y=0$
  • y-intercept $(0,1)$; no x-intercept
  • $b>1$: grows · $0
  • One-to-one: $b^a=b^c \Rightarrow a=c$

Transformations of $b^x$

  • $b^{x+c}$ shift left $c$ · $b^{x-c}$ shift right $c$
  • $b^x+k$ shift up $k$ — asymptote $y=k$
  • $-b^x$ reflect over x-axis (range flips)
  • $b^{-x}$ reflect over y-axis

Logarithm definition

$x=b^y \;\Longleftrightarrow\; y=\log_b x$

  • $\log x=\log_{10}x$ · $\ln x=\log_e x$
  • $\log_b b^x=x$ · $b^{\log_b x}=x$
  • $\log 10^x=x$ · $\ln e^x=x$ · $10^{\log x}=x$ · $e^{\ln x}=x$

Log graph $y=\log_b x$

  • Domain $(0,\infty)$, Range $(-\infty,\infty)$
  • Vertical asymptote $x=0$
  • x-intercept $(1,0)$; no y-intercept
  • For $y=\log_b(\text{arg})$: domain ← set arg $>0$; VA ← set arg $=0$; x-int ← set arg $=1$ (shift-free)

Key Examples from the Packet

  • Ex 1: Graph $y_1=3^x$ & $y_2=2^x$ on same axes — $y_1$ above $y_2$ for $x>0$.
  • Ex 2: $y=3^{x+1}+1$: asymptote $y=1$, y-int $(0,4)$, left shift 1, up 1.
  • Ex 4: $4^{-2}=\tfrac1{16} \Leftrightarrow \log_4 \tfrac1{16}=-2$.
  • Ex 5: $\log_x 64=2 \Rightarrow x=8$. $\log_2 \tfrac18=-3$. $\log 100=2$. $\ln 8=3\ln 2$.
  • Ex 6: $e^{\ln 4}=4$, $\log 10^3=3$, $\ln e^2=2$.
  • Ex 8: Domain $\log_3(2+x)$ → $x>-2$. $\log_2(4-x)$ → $x<4$. $\log_3(2x-5)+1$ → $x>5/2$.
  • Ex 9: VAs: $x=-2$, $x=4$, $x=5/2$ respectively.
